About Us

Infrastructure

The company is an exclusive division for manufacturing home furnishing fabrics .It lives by design and product creation for this segment of the market.

We specialize in

  • Basic plains in grey state and piece dyed.
  • Traditional plains and dobby structures in yarn dyed.
  • Embroidery, Prints and Crewel work and combinations.
  • Yarn dyed wovens in stripes and checks.
  • Yarn dyed woven in stripes and checks.
  • Jacquards.
  • Tropical treatments-FR, UV protection, water repellency etc., meeting the requirement standards of the global market.

The monthly production of these fabrics is about 45,000 Mtrs

Quality

The company pursues continuous improvement in its product quality and productivity to achieve complete customer satisfaction. We maintain good colour fastness standards in line with quality standards of various countries. The Quality System is constantly reviewed, studied and upgraded to customer requirements.

Team

We employ "NO CHILD LABOUR" and the team compromises of talented designers and technical professionals who have been in the textile manufacturing and product development model of our business. A good customer service and support team takes care of the timely needs of our customers.
